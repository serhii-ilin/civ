## Next Iteration Notes

The following areas were identified during generation as requiring attention in the next iteration:

1. **Simulation execution**: The most important missing piece. All simulation artifacts are specifications; actual simulation runs are needed.

2. **Evidence-matrix expansion**: Some architecture artifacts contain implicit empirical claims not yet in the evidence matrix. Full audit needed.

3. **Cross-module consistency audit**: Systematic review of all module interfaces for consistency.

4. **Behavioral transition model**: The framework assumes behavioral conditions (high trust, cooperation, civic engagement) that are not universal. A model of how to transition from low-trust baseline is needed.

5. **Catastrophic+ scenarios**: Current stress scenarios are capped at "severe" or "catastrophic." Scenarios for truly worst-case conditions (3C+ warming, cascading global collapse) are needed.

6. **External independent review**: All content generated by single agent. Independent review by different agent or human expert is essential.

7. **Liberty-impact assessments**: Systematic accounting of liberty restrictions and justifications.

8. **Counterfactual historical analysis**: For each major architectural choice, analyze the closest historical analogue that failed.
