# ART-029: Contradiction Register

Iteration: `baseline-v0.1`
Status: draft

## Purpose

Document internal contradictions and tensions within the framework. Contradictions are not bugs -- they reflect genuine tensions in any complex social design. They are preserved rather than prematurely resolved, with resolution options and evidence requirements specified.

## Unresolved vs. Managed

Contradictions marked "Managed" have a chosen architecture resolution but remain live tensions. They are managed, not solved. Adversarial review and simulation may surface conditions under which management fails.

Contradictions marked "Open" require more evidence, simulation results, or design iteration.

## Contradiction Handling Protocol

Per `canon/evidence-standard.md`:

1. Assign contradiction ID ✓ (this register)
2. Name affected modules and values ✓
3. Explain without premature resolution ✓
4. Propose resolution options ✓
5. Identify evidence needed ✓
6. Record final decision only after rationale explicit -- pending for open contradictions
