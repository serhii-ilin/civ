# Failure Mode Register

Artifact ID: ART-006
Iteration: baseline-v0.1
Status: draft
Change type: new
Owner module: MOD-RES
Depends on: ART-003, ART-004

## Purpose

Catalog of the dominant failure modes for human societies, with historical examples, plausible mechanisms, candidate structural mitigations, residual risk after mitigation, and the stress tests that should challenge the framework's claimed defenses. These IDs feed the risk register (ART-030), the simulation stress-test scenarios (ART-021), and the architecture modules. Mitigations are proposals at this stage; their effectiveness is contingent on the design choices in ART-009 through ART-018.

## Cross-Cutting Patterns

Many failures share patterns that the framework's overall architecture must address structurally rather than module-by-module:

1. **Power concentration through legal but corrosive channels**: RSK-001, RSK-002, RSK-023, RSK-024, RSK-030. Mitigation: redundant authority distribution, sortition bodies, transparency.
2. **Feedback suppression**: RSK-003, RSK-019, RSK-020, RSK-043. Mitigation: independent observation, adversarial review, whistleblower protection, decision-log discipline.
3. **Externalization of costs over time or onto powerless groups**: RSK-016, RSK-026, RSK-038. Mitigation: future-generations advocacy, polluter-pays, environmental justice.
4. **Ratcheting of exceptional powers**: RSK-002, RSK-009, RSK-034, RSK-035. Mitigation: hard sunset clauses, mandatory review, expiration without reauthorization.
5. **Brittleness from premature uniformity**: RSK-021, RSK-029, RSK-032. Mitigation: redundancy and variation within shared constraints.
6. **Latency between weak signal and institutional response**: RSK-003, RSK-007, RSK-008, RSK-020. Mitigation: monitoring infrastructure with public reporting, rehearsed crisis playbooks.

## Open Issues

- RSK-010 (AI alignment) is poorly served by simulation alone; pair with adversarial review and red-team exercises.
- RSK-041 (geoengineering) is largely outside the framework's unilateral control; mitigation depends on international coordination.
- RSK-038 (unhealed historical harms) requires participatory work that this artifact cannot specify abstractly.
- The register currently under-represents intersectional risks; future iterations should add compound-scenario rows.
