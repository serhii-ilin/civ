# MOD-LAW: Law and Justice

## Purpose

Define the legal order, rights protections, public safety system, and justice mechanisms required for a legitimate society.

## Design Requirements

- Constitutional rights and civil liberties.
- Due process, appeals, legal representation, and transparent standards.
- Independent courts and review bodies.
- Public safety institutions with anti-abuse safeguards.
- Restorative, rehabilitative, and protective justice pathways.
- Clear limits on emergency powers.
- Protection for dissent, whistleblowing, journalism, association, and minority groups.
- Accessible dispute resolution at local and higher scales.
- Legal rules that distinguish member observability of public processes from prohibited surveillance of private life.

## Core Questions

- RQ-LAW-001: What rights are non-derogable even during emergency?
- RQ-LAW-002: How are policing, investigation, and public safety designed to prevent abuse?
- RQ-LAW-003: What offenses require separation from the community, and under what review?
- RQ-LAW-004: How are restorative and punitive responses balanced?
- RQ-LAW-005: What legal mechanisms prevent institutional capture?
- RQ-LAW-006: What observability rights do members have, and what due-process standards govern restricted information?
