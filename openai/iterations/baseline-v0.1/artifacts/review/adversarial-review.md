## Findings

| Finding ID | Perspective | Severity | Finding | Affected Artifacts | Proposed Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ADV-001 | Skeptic | High | The framework may overestimate how much complexity ordinary participants and institutions can process. | ART-009, ART-018 | Add simplification reviews, user testing, and decision-latency thresholds. |
| ADV-002 | Historian | High | Mixed regimes often fail when economic inequality captures military, courts, or agenda-setting channels. | ART-009, ART-010, ART-017 | Deepen anti-oligarchy design and simulate elite capture. |
| ADV-003 | Behavioral scientist | High | The design relies on high trust, civic motivation, and compliance during stress, which may decay quickly after failure. | ART-009, ART-012, ART-014 | Model trust asymmetry and repair mechanisms. |
| ADV-004 | Institutional-abuse reviewer | High | Observability infrastructure can become a surveillance tool unless privacy architecture is enforceable and technically constrained. | ART-016, ART-018 | Define data schemas, access controls, and abuse penalties before pilots. |
| ADV-005 | Civil-liberties reviewer | High | Emergency, health, defense, and AI exceptions could swallow rights if review bodies defer to officials. | ART-011, ART-012, ART-016, ART-017 | Add independent counsel, expiration, compensation, and public case summaries. |
| ADV-006 | Ecological-systems reviewer | High | Ecological caps are asserted but not calibrated to specific bioregions or technologies. | ART-015, ART-020 | Build regional ecological budgets and uncertainty ranges. |
| ADV-007 | Economist | Medium | Universal services, reserves, and redundancy may be unaffordable without strong productivity and tax compliance. | ART-010, ART-024 | Fiscal model and phased service guarantees. |
| ADV-008 | Implementation reviewer | Medium | A pilot could become an ideological enclave rather than a broad civic test. | ART-025, ART-026 | Require pluralist onboarding, independent evaluation, and exit protections. |

## Residual Risk

The highest residual risks are elite capture, surveillance drift, ecological cap failure, emergency normalization, and fiscal underfunding of commons infrastructure.
