Iteration: `baseline-v0.1`

Status: `draft`

Module: `MOD-HMH`

## Design Thesis

Health is treated as civic infrastructure. The system integrates prevention, clinical care, public health, mental health, disability support, family support, crisis response, and built-environment design.

Evidence links: CLM-007, CLM-008, CLM-016, CLM-017, CLM-018, CLM-020.

## Guaranteed Services

| Scale | Services |
| --- | --- |
| Household | Health literacy, emergency kits, telehealth access, preventive reminders, caregiver respite access. |
| Community cell | Primary care clinic, mental health first response, social-work navigation, peer support, elder and disability support, isolation checks with consent. |
| Regional | Hospitals, specialty care, inpatient psychiatric care, addiction services, rehabilitation, public-health laboratory, mobile crisis teams. |
| Constitutional tier | Universal coverage rules, health data standards, epidemic coordination, drug and device regulation, rights safeguards. |

## Mental Health Design

- Normalize low-friction access to counseling, peer support, crisis care, and psychiatric treatment.
- Avoid routing every social distress case through law enforcement.
- Use trauma-informed defaults in schools, courts, care systems, and public agencies.
- Track wait times, suicide attempts, overdose events, involuntary holds, loneliness indicators, and treatment continuity.
- Protect privacy by reporting aggregate community health signals rather than personal exposure.

## Public Health Powers

Public health authority can use testing, isolation, vaccination campaigns, business restrictions, and emergency procurement only under legal necessity, proportionality, evidence review, expiration, appeal where practical, and compensation where burdens are concentrated.

## Built Environment

Health design requirements:

- Walkable access to food, care, schools, public space, and transit.
- Housing quality standards for air, light, heat, safety, crowding, and noise.
- Community spaces that support intergenerational contact without forced participation.
- Green and blue infrastructure for heat, flood, recreation, and mental health benefits.
