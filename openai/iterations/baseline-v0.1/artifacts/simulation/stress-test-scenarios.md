# Stress-Test Scenarios

Iteration: `baseline-v0.1`

Status: `draft`

## Scenarios

| Scenario ID | Scenario | Primary Modules | Failure Signals |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| SCN-001 | Multi-year drought plus food price shock. | ECOINF, ECO, HMH, DEF | Food access below floor, water stress breach, unrest, emergency overreach. |
| SCN-002 | Severe cyberattack on grid, health records, and payment systems. | TAI, DEF, GOV, HMH | Critical-service outage beyond target, data breach, loss of public trust. |
| SCN-003 | Elite capture through procurement, media ownership, and campaign finance. | GOV, ECO, LAW, TAI | Audit failure, concentrated contracts, policy skew, rights complaints. |
| SCN-004 | Pandemic with uncertain early evidence. | HMH, LAW, GOV, DEF | Delayed response, overbroad restrictions, health-system overload, rights violations. |
| SCN-005 | Migration shock after regional conflict. | DEF, CUL, HMH, EDU, ECO | Housing overload, discrimination, service backlog, informal labor exploitation. |
| SCN-006 | Automation displacement in major sectors. | ECO, EDU, TAI, CUL | Unemployment, reskilling failure, wealth concentration, status conflict. |
| SCN-007 | Local community becomes coercive and exclusionary. | CUL, LAW, GOV, HMH | Exit obstruction, discrimination, hidden abuse, local capture. |
| SCN-008 | Ecological accounting ignored during growth boom. | ECOINF, ECO, GOV | Boundary breach, infrastructure stress, hidden liabilities. |
| SCN-009 | External military coercion and disinformation. | DEF, GOV, TAI, LAW | Emergency normalization, secrecy expansion, civic polarization. |
| SCN-010 | Fiscal downturn plus infrastructure decay. | ECO, ECOINF, GOV | Maintenance backlog, service quality loss, trust decline. |

## Required Scenario Outputs

Each run must report:

- Metrics crossed.
- Values affected.
- Assumptions invalidated.
- Decisions requiring revisit.
- Whether failure is institutional, resource, behavioral, ecological, security, or model-design related.
